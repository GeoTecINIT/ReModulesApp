One click diagnosis

To reach the 2050 decarbonisation targets, most European buildings require renovation. Yet, many supply- and demand-side barriers prevent building renovation from reaching the necessary scale in the residential sector. One click diagnosis makes deep renovations easier, faster and more attractive for stakeholders by providing a quick but reliable automated diagnosis on the building actual performance and the renovation options available to be engaged in the renovation process. From this starting point, users will be given access to other key results of relevant European projects to facilitate decision making on deep renovation.
